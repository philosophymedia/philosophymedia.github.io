About

The Timeless Wisdom of Rock and Roll

Rock stars are philosophers and truthtellers. We need their wisdom more than ever.
Right now, everyone is searching for answers to the big questions in life. We are in a rare moment in which information is everywhere, but the world has lost both its compass and its trusted narrative voice. Real wisdom from a trusted source is in short supply. At a time like this, the world needs an authentic voice capable of cutting through the noise.

Enter The Philosophy of Rock & Roll. Great songs can help to answer those big questions, and rock stars (past and present) are some of our most important philosophers and truthtellers. Their version of universal wisdom and truth is expressed simply and packaged for mass consumption in the most popular songs of our time.
